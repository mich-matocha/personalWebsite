Me

Michaela Matocha

Howdy! I'm Michaela, as you might have gathered, and this is my website. I'm a New York born gal (I've only lived in Texas since 2014) and am a proud member of the fightin' Texas Aggie class of 2022! I'm currently working my way to a computer science degree, and I'm super excited to break into industry to make my mark on the world.

Outside of classes, I'm one of the SWElites co-chairs for the Society of Women Engineers. SWElites is essentially a leadership program for freshmen, and we help develop the fifty women as college students and professionals. All in all, it's pretty cool and I get to meet a ton of new people through it. I've also recently joined Texas Aggie Game Developers, but not much has come of that yet, so updates to come!

I love my dog, Sadie, a border collie mix, but she lives at home with my family. For fun I like to crochet, cross stitch, and occasionally make friendship bracelets. I also love video games, cheese, and podcasts. As I mentioned on the homepage, I love board games. A few of my favorites are Exploding Kittens, Machi Koro, and Power Grid.
